  • First reason

    Tests will give you confidence in your knowledge.

  • Second reason

    Use tests to master the material.

    You can test your knowledge of a large amount of material in a short time

  • Third reason

    Objectivity and accuracy.

    The test form of verification allows you to remove the human factor (subjective or biased opinion of the teacher, personal attitude, poor health), and the developed grading scale and clear criteria for answers and grades give an accurate result and make the test result transparent for all participants in the educational process (even if the test checks the student himself, his friend or another teacher).

  • Card Four

    Understandability and expectability

    The student himself can predict the result and prepare in advance, which makes tests less stressful than an oral exam. And most importantly, the student himself can calculate the possible number of points and his own result in advance, as well as perform those tasks that will bring him the maximum number of points.
